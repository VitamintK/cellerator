CellzillaICToList CelleratorML Home

Converts a list of initial conditions from standard Cellzilla form to list form.

Needs["CelleratorML`"];

CellzillaICToList[{a[1]→val,a[2]→val,..,b[1]→val,b[2]→val, ..}]

returns a list

{a→{val,val,..}, b→{val, val,..}, ..}
